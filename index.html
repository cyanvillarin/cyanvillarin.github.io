Greetings!

I am an iOS Developer with over 6 years of professional experience in developing customer-facing iOS apps using Swift. I like to work on iOS apps that has a great impact on people’s lives, because that would bring me great satisfaction while developing for those apps. I also like working on apps which have a large user base, because that makes me want to be a perfectionist and release apps with no bugs.

I’m also an advocate of Agile Scrum since I believe in doing things step by step! I personally love anything related to health and sports. I believe that a person’s greatest asset is his/her health that’s why there’s a saying Health is Wealth. Other things that interest me are things related to dreams, universe, and fishes.

And lastly, I also have interests in participating in UI/UX design, as well as I have a little bit of experience in database designing and backend development.

I also have some iOS Apps that can be downloaded from the App Store. All apps are available only on the iOS platform both for iPhone and iPad devices.
