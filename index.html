Our apps are meant to inspire people

At Inspiralists, we develop apps that are meant to inspire people around the globe. Most of our apps are ad-supported, and can be downloaded from the App Store. All apps are available only on the iOS platform both for iPhone and iPad devices.

Chatzone

This app is a secured disappearing messages app. Use it to send sensitive information (passwords, personal information like TIN, credit card numbers, addresses, etc) to other people without worrying about your messages being saved somewhere else.

UAAP Sports

UAAP Sports is an iOS app that displays the updated standings of teams playing in the current season (or last season if there's no ongoing games). It also allow users to see who are the most viewed players in the league. This app is plainly a fan app designed to those who want to be updated with the teams' scores and players' information.

Emotions

This app allows the user to see how other people are feeling. It also displays a quote that is relevant to the emotion that he/she has selected.

Happisa

This app is just a simple app in which you can answer if you are happy or not. You also choose to input you the reason as to why you chose that answer. Lastly, you will be able to see other people's reasons as to why they're happy or not
